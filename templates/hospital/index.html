How much you charge for consultancy

Our consultancy charges start at ₹500, depending on the specialist and nature of the consultation. The exact fee may vary based on the type of service, whether it's an initial visit, follow-up, or specialized evaluation. Please contact our reception desk or visit our website for detailed pricing and appointment booking.

What kind of patients do you treat?

We treat a wide range of patients with various heart-related conditions, including those with high blood pressure, coronary artery disease, heart rhythm disorders, congenital heart defects, and those recovering from heart surgery. Whether it's preventive care, diagnosis, or advanced cardiac treatment, we offer comprehensive services for patients of all age groups.

Where does my refering Cardiologist go?

Your referring cardiologist remains an important part of your care team. We collaborate closely with referring doctors to ensure a smooth transition and continuity of care. After your evaluation or treatment with us, we share detailed reports and updates with your cardiologist so that your long-term care remains coordinated and consistent.
